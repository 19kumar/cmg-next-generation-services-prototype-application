You might need us to collect child maintenance for you

This service is called 'Collect and Pay'. You will only be able to use this service if the other parent agrees or if it's unlikely that the other parent will pay maintenance.

What it costs to use Collect and Pay

To use Collect and Pay, you'll need to pay:

  • a 4% fee from your regular child maintenance payment if you’re receiving child maintenance
  • a 20% fee on top of your regular child maintenance payment if you’re paying child maintenance

Examples

If you're receiving child maintenance
Child maintenance arrangement £200 a month
Collect and Pay fee £8 a month
Total child maintenance received £192 a month
If you're paying child maintenance
Child maintenance arrangement £200 a month
Collect and Pay fee £40 a month
Total child maintenance paid £240 a month
