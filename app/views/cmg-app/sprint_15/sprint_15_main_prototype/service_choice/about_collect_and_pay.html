If you do not think Direct Pay will work for you

We may be able to collect payments from the other parent and pass them on to you. This is called 'Collect and Pay'.

We will:

  • work out how much the payments will be and tell both parents
  • collect payments from the other parent and pass them on to you – there are charges for this
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change
We need to ask the other parent how they want to pay.

When you can use Collect and Pay

You can only use Collect and Pay if the other parent:

  • refuses to respond to us
  • has missed previous payments (and there is evidence of this)
  • agrees to use Collect and Pay
Collect and Pay charges Amount to pay Example
When you receive child maintenance 4% is taken from each payment. You'd get £96 from a £100 payment.
When the other parent pays child maintenance 20% is added to each payment. They'd pay £20 on top of a £100 payment. They'd pay £120 in total.
