We need to ask the other parent how they want to pay child maintenance

The other parent might ask us to collect payments from them and pass these on to you. This is called 'Collect and Pay'.

If the other parent chooses Collect and Pay, we will:

  • work out how much maintenance must be paid and tell both parents
  • collect payments from the other parent and pass them on to you – there are charges for this
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change
Collect and Pay charges Amount you pay Example
If you receive child maintenance 4% taken from each payment You'd lose £4 from a £100 payment
If you pay child maintenance 20% on top of each payment You'd pay £20 on top of a £100 payment
