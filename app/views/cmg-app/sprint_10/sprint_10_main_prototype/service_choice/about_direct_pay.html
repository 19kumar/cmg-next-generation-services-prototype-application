How you receive child maintenance

Most separated parents who use the Child Maintenance Service start with a ‘Direct Pay’ arrangement. This is where the other parent makes payments direct to your bank account.

We will:

  • not charge you ongoing fees to use Direct Pay
  • work out how much maintenance must be paid, and tell both parents
  • give both parents a payment schedule
  • review your calculation each year, or if either parent’s circumstances change

If you do not want contact with the other parent

! Warning We will give them your bank details so they can set up payments.

If payments are not made

You can report payments that are more than 5 days late to us. We may change your arrangement type, and can take legal action if payments are not made.
