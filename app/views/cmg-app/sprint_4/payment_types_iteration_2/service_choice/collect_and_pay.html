Collect and Pay is the best option for you, reseaons why...

The Child Maintenance Service might be able to collect child maintenance from the other parent and pass it on to you. This is called 'Collect and Pay'.

To use it, you’ll need to pay:

  • a 4% fee from your regular child maintenance payment if you’re receiving child maintenance
  • a 20% fee on top of your regular child maintenance payment if you’re paying child maintenance
