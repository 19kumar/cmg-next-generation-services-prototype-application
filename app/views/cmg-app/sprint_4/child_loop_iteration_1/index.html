Apply to the Child Maintenance Service

Use this service to get help making a child maintenance arrangement if you and the other parent cannot agree to one yourselves.

You’ll need:

  • the invitation code we sent in the email about applying to the Child Maintenance Service
  • your National Insurance number
  • the other parent's name
  • the date any child maintenance court order started
Your name, location and other personal information will not be shared with the other parent.

There’s usually a £20 application fee. You’ll need a debit or credit card to pay this.

Start now

Before you start

Give as much information as you can about the other parent – it will help us to process your application faster. This could include:

  • where they live
  • their National Insurance number
  • their date of birth

Check if you’re eligible to apply to the Child Maintenance Service.

You can arrange child maintenance yourselves, if you can agree.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

If you need more than one child maintenance arrangement

You should speak to the Child Maintenance Options team if you need to claim from more than one parent.
