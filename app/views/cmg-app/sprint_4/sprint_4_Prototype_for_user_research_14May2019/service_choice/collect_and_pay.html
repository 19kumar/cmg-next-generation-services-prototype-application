You can only use Collect and Pay if the other parent is unlikely to pay on time

We need to ask the other parent how they want to pay child maintenance. If there's no evidence they have missed any previous payments, we will usually set up a Direct Pay arrangement.

You do not need to have any contact with the other parent if you use Direct Pay.

What it costs to use Collect and Pay

Amount you pay Example
If you receive child maintenance 4% taken from each payment You'd lose £4 from a £100 payment
If you pay child maintenance 20% on top of each payment You'd pay £20 on top of a £100 payment
