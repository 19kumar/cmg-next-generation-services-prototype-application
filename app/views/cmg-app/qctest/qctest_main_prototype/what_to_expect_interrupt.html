What to expect

We will ask you questions about yourself, your children and how you want child maintenance to be paid.

Helpful information about the other parent

We need information about the other parent so we can set up your child maintenance application. You will need to give their name and as much other information as you can. This could include:

  • their phone number
  • any details about where they live or work
  • their date of birth or age
  • their National Insurance number
If you do not know all of these details you can still apply but it might take longer to set up your application.

Setting up an online account

As part of your application, we will set up an online child maintenance account for you. We will ask you to create a personal identification number (PIN), a password and give your email address.

Only you and our team will be able to see the security details you create.
