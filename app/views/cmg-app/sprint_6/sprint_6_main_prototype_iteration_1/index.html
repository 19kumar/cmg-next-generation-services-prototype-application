Apply to the Child Maintenance Service

Use this service to get help making a child maintenance arrangement if you and the other parent cannot agree to one yourselves.

You can arrange child maintenance yourselves, if you can agree.

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • your unique invitation code – this is in the email you were sent after speaking to Child Maintenance Options
  • your National Insurance number
  • your bank account details
  • details of the children you're applying for
  • the other parent's name
There’s usually a £20 application fee. You’ll need a debit or credit card to pay this.

If you do not want contact with the other parent

We will contact them to set up an arrangement. Only your name and children's names will be shared with them.

If we cannot find the other parent

We might not be able to set up an arrangement. Helpful information includes:

  • their date of birth or age
  • any details about where they live
  • their phone number
  • where they work
  • their National Insurance number
Your bank account details can be protected or shared with the other parent to help make an arrangement. You can speak to your bank or building society about getting an account that does not give away your location – we call this a 'non-geographic account'.

Applying by phone

You should speak to Child Maintenance Options if:

  • you need to claim from more than one parent
  • you do not have an email address
  • you do not have your own phone number
