What happens next

We will:

  • send you a text message with your customer reference number in the next 24 hours
  • send you an information pack about the Child Maintenance Service
  • write to you with details of your application
  • try to contact the other parent using the information you've given
  • confirm the information we have is correct and collect any other information we need from them
  • work out how much child maintenance they need to pay and when to pay it

How long your application will take

Applications usually take about 6 weeks.

Your application will take longer if we have problems contacting the other parent.

The other parent will become responsible for paying child maintenance within 7 days of us first contacting them. It might take longer for you to receive your first payment.

Contacting us about your case

You can tell us about changes to your case online. You'll need to sign in to your online child maintenance account using:

  • your customer reference number
  • your National Insurance number
  • your personal identification number (PIN)

You can also call us about your case.
