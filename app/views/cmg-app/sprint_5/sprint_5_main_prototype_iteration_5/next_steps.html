What happens next

We will process your application and contact you if we need any more information.

We will also:

  • send you an information pack with details of how the Child Maintenance Service can help

How long your application will take

Applications usually take 4 to 6 weeks. Your child maintenance claim will not start until we have contacted the other parent. After we have contacted them, we will write to you both with details of when and how much child maintenance will be paid.

Your application might take longer if we cannot contact the other parent.
