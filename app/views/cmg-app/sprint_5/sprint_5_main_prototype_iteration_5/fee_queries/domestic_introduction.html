We need to make sure you can get the right support

To do this we need to ask you some sensitive questions about domestic abuse. The answers you give will help you to get the right support at the end of this application.

Your answers will never be shared with the other parent or reported to authorities like the police or social services.

What is domestic abuse?

Domestic abuse is controlling, forceful, threatening, degrading or violent behaviour. Anybody can be an abuser, including a partner, ex-partner or family member. It is very common.
