What we will ask in the following questions

The following question is about abuse you may have experienced in your previous relationship with the other parent.

The reason why we ask this question relates to how we handle your case and offer you the correct support at the end of the application.

You will then be asked a series of questions about you, your children and the other parent, this on average can take up to 45 minutes.

This information will help us identify the relevant people and offer you the best chance of a successful case.
