How child maintenance is paid

Direct Pay

Most separated parents who use the Child Maintenance Service manage payments between themselves. This is called Direct Pay.

A Direct Pay arrangement means the Child Maintenance Service will:

  • calculate the amount of maintenance that must be paid, and tell both parents
  • take legal action if payments are not made
  • make a new calculation each year, or if either parentâ€™s circumstances change
! Warning If you do not want to have contact with the other parent, the Child Maintenance Service can contact them for you. We will provide them with your bank details so payments can be set up.

If the paying parent refuses to pay child maintenance, the Child Maintenance Service can change your arrangement type in the future.
