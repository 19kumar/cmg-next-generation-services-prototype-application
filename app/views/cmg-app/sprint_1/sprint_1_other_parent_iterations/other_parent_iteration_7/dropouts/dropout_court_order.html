You are not eligible to apply to the Child Maintenance Service

The Child Maintenance Service cannot help if there is already a court order, consent order or minute of agreement for child maintenance in place that:

  • was made before 3 March 2003
  • their National Insurance number
  • has been in place for less than 12 months

You may be able to change or enforce an existing court order or consent order through the courts.
