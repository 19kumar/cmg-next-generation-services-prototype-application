How would you like child maintenance to be paid?

Select your preferred option. The other parent will be given a choice too.
The Child Maintenance Service can work out how much child maintenance should be paid and tell both parents. This is called 'Direct Pay'.
The Child Maintenance Service might be able to collect child maintenance from the other parent and pass it on to you. This is called 'Collect and Pay'. You'll need to pay to use Collect and Pay.
What does it cost to get the Child Maintenance Service to collect payments?

You’ll need to pay:

  • a 4% fee from your regular child maintenance payment if you’re receiving child maintenance
  • a 20% fee on top of your regular child maintenance payment if you’re paying child maintenance
