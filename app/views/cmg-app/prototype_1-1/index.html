Apply to the Child Maintenance Service

Use this service to get help making a child maintenance arrangement if you and the other parent cannot agree to one yourselves.

You’ll need the:

  • invitation code we sent in the email about applying to the Child Maintenance Service
  • other parent's name
  • date any child maintenance court order started
  • bank account details you want child maintenance paid into

There’s usually a £20 application fee. You’ll need a debit or credit card to pay this.

Your personal details will not be shared with the other parent.
Start now

Before you start

Give as much information as you can about the other parent. This could include:

  • where they live
  • their National Insurance number
  • their date of birth

Check if you’re eligible to apply to the Child Maintenance Service.

You can arrange child maintenance yourselves, if you can agree.

This service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).
