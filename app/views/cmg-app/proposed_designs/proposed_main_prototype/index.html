Apply to use the Child Maintenance Service

Use this service to set up a child maintenance arrangement if you and the other parent cannot agree to one yourselves.

Before you start

You’ll need:

  • your unique invitation code – this is in the email you were sent after speaking to Child Maintenance Options
  • your National Insurance number
  • your bank account details
  • details of the children you're applying for
  • the other parent's name
There’s usually a £20 application fee. You’ll need a debit or credit card to pay this.
Start now

When you cannot apply online

You should call us if:

  • you need child maintenance for more than 4 children
  • you need child maintenance from more than one parent
