You've sent your application to the Child Maintenance Service. We have sent you a confirmation email.

What happens next

We will process your application and contact you if we need any more information.

We will also:

  • send you an information pack with details of how the Child Maintenance Service can help
  • send you a 'scheme customer reference number' – you'll need this to contact us about your arrangment
  • contact the other parent – your personal details will not be shared with them
Your application may take longer if we cannot contact the other parent.

We will then send you and the other parent details of how much child maintenance should be paid.
